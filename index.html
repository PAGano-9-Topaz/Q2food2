leche-flan

Step 1: Seperate white and yolks

Step 2: beat egg yolks in a bowl

Step 3: add condensed milk - mix

Step 4: add fresh/evap milk and vanilla - mix

Step 5: put mold on stove - low heat

Step 6: put sugar on mold till caramel

Step 7: pour egg yolk and milk mixture on mold

Step 8: cover top of mold using aluminum foil

Step 9: steam the mixture for 32 minutes

Step 10: Let it cool, then serve

Ingredients

10 eggs‎ ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎ ‎

1 can condensed milk

1 cup fresh/evaporated milk

1 cup granulated sugar

1 tsp vanilla extract
